How It All Began

Our Story

A love written in the stars

Our story started during our housemanship at OAU Teaching Hospital, Ile-Ife, when we were both posted to Paediatrics. In the middle of busy ward rounds, long calls, and everything that comes with hospital life, we somehow found each other.

What began as casual conversations after rounds soon became something we both looked forward to — gist-filled walks around the hospital, laughing over the smallest things. It felt easy, like we had known each other much longer.

Friendship came first, strong and genuine. Then somewhere along the line, we took a chance on us. From our first dates to all the little moments in between, our love grew steadily, beautifully, and with intention.

What started in Ile-Ife is now a lifelong journey. With our families, our culture, and God at the centre — we can't wait to say "I do."

Travel Tips
  • 🕐 Lagos traffic can be intense — please plan 1–2 hours for any journey during rush hour
  • 🚗 Bolt and Uber are the safest and most affordable way to get around
  • ✈️ International guests — fly into Murtala Muhammed Airport (LOS)
  • 💱 The local currency is the Nigerian Naira (₦) — most places accept card payments and bank transfers

Things to Do in Lagos

Lekki Conservation Centre →
Walk Africa's longest canopy walkway above the treetops and spot monkeys, birds and more.
 🎨
Nike Art Gallery →
Five floors of stunning Nigerian art and crafts — one of West Africa's largest galleries.
 🏖️
Tarkwa Bay Beach →
A sheltered, boat-access beach perfect for swimming and a relaxed afternoon by the water.
 🏖️
Elegushi Beach →
One of Lagos' most popular beaches — live music, seafood, bonfires and stunning sunsets right by the reception venue.
 🎭

Lagos' premier arts venue — live theatre, exhibitions, a bookshop and a lovely restaurant with Nigerian and continental dishes.
